    What is this project about?

    We start with one of my favorite tools, a whiteboard. Standing in front of the whiteboard, our team will ask questions to get you talking about what you want your new software/app/website to do, how you want it to look and what you are trying to accomplish. At this point we focus mainly on the ideas and we don’t get into the details. For example: If you where building a social networking site you might say “I would like the system to notify everyone in my group when they have been assigned a new task”. It’s unnecessary at this point to ask what you want the notification to say or what color the text should be. We save those details for when we are building that piece of the program.

    Which pieces do you want right know?

    Since we will be delivering fully functioning pieces of your project every three weeks, it’s important to put these pieces in order of priority. The next step of our process is helping you to get the most important functionality to the top of the list. A good way to get started is to consider which three pieces you would have developed if you couldn’t have any more.

    Continuous feedback

    Now that we know which pieces to work on first, we will be asking for the details needed to get your software done. At the end of three weeks you will have something that you can start using that day.

    Hmm, this is not exactly what I wanted

    This is good news. “What?” Yes, good news. Since we have only built a few small pieces and only spent three weeks on building it, we are most likely not too far off from what you need. The good news is that it is so early in the project that we can easily make adjustments. Each time we adjust, we get a better understanding of what you really want. And so do you.

    Hey, I want to add more functionality!!!!

    This is what we do the best. We are experts at building custom software that is super flexible. That means that we can add things easily without breaking what is already there.
