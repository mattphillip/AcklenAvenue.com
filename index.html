    // SERVICES

    1
    agile coaching and mentoring

    Interested in bringing Agile to your team? Why not have an Agile coach help you implement the fundamentals? We don’t just assess the situation, we stick around and get the process moving.

    We work with your business analysts to bring clarity to your requirements. We work with your project manager to bring clarity to your process. We work with your developers to bring clarity to your code. All of this adds up to very focused and disciplined progress.

    2
    custom software development

    Do you have a complex problem to solve? Do you need a custom web application or cross platform mobile application? We have the knowledge, experience and resources to handle every aspect of your project from start to finish.

    Acklen Avenue specializes in writing highly maintainable code and strictly adheres to Test Driven Development (a development process recommended by the leaders of the Agile community). We also have a disciplined approach to core Agile Software Development practices which enable the delivery of fully functioning pieces of your project every two to three weeks.

    3
    near-shore staff augmentation

    With Acklen Avenue’s CTO Byron Sommardahl living part of the year in Honduras, it made perfect sense to find the smartest and most tenacious developers, train them in TDD and core Agile practices and invite them to join our team.

    Our Honduran developers are very similar culturally to their Nashville teammates and are in the same time zone. That coupled with the fact that our CTO works directly with each member, makes Acklen Avenue the perfect solution to your remote Agile development needs.
