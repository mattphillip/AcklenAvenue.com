    Sector: Health care

    Issue: Critical research data jeopardized

    Solution: Custom software development

    A frightfully high rate of infection also makes Haiti an epicenter for AIDS research, but blood analysis machines could not store much data from blood samples without overwriting it. With a test every 5 minutes on weekdays, reams of data were at risk - each sample involved up to 100s of data points. For this Vanderbilt-funded research effort, Acklen Avenue created distinct pieces of software, one for each type of machine, to extract information into a single database. Researchers now have access to historical data and can track trends and produce reports with ease.

    Sector: Health care

    Issue: Inability to scale or share P4P program

    Solution: Custom software development

    The head of a Florida hospital system had developed a fair, accurate system to track pay-for-performance metrics. But the “system” relied on an old-fashioned Excel spreadsheet that only he understood how to use. Acklen Avenue transformed this great idea from a complicated, time-consuming task into automated software that also creates a potential revenue source for our client. Our solution allows administrators to set objectives and provides a dashboard for staff to see. With the healthcare sector’s emphasis on P4P, PiiMs plans to license the program to other systems.

    Sector: Health care

    Issue: Technical debt burdened existing software

    Solution: Agile coaching, Staff Augmentation

    Acklen Avenue worked side-by-side InVivoLink developers to improve software for orthopedic surgeons and their staff and mentor the company’s internal team on best practices. Features include automated research, a Level 3 implant registry, surgeon preferences and integrated ordering. Other components improve patient outcomes and reporting for performance-based payments as well as quality and safety. Technical debt had rendered existing software nearly unable to incorporate new features. Now, InVivo Link’s developers can add features more quickly and at less expense.

    Sector: Technology

    Issue: Integration issues limited product’s reach

    Solution: Custom software development

    Acklen Avenue expanded LeanKit’s potential client base by integrating its popular project management and real-time collaboration system with other APIs. Our program stands between LeanKit and other apps, translating back-and-forth. We built the hub, handled the integration with Google Docs and JIRA and made it easy for other developers to customize LeanKit to work with other APIs. So, for example, if a client’s IT staff prefers LeanKit but the marketing department uses Basecamp, everyone stays happy. The flexible software opens unlimited doors for LeanKit and its customers.
